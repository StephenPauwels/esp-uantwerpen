Registration Info

In order to be covered by the University of Antwerp insurance, it is mandatory to register your internship according to the proper procedure. Start the registration through SisA (Request Mobility/Internship)
Please make sure to finalise the registration procedure (including the upload of all mandatory documents) at the latest 2 weeks before the actual start of your internship. The mandatory documents are generated through this application process. If your registration is not finalised before the actual start of your traineeship, your insurance will not be valid and you are NOT allowed to start the internship.
